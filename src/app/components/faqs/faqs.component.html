Our Care Services

Choosing the right type of care for you and your loved one is a significant decision. We offer many benefits, such as the ability to remain in your own home and maintain preferred routines, whether it's through regular visits from a domiciliary carer or live-in care arrangements. Our person-centred planning ensures that every care package is tailored to the individual, whether you need support for a few hours a week or 24-hour care. Our carers assist with a variety of needs, from personal care like washing and dressing to domestic tasks such as cleaning and meal preparation. The amount of care required depends on several factors, including medical conditions, mobility, availability of support from family and friends, personal preferences, and budget. We consider your loved one’s wishes at every step to ensure they feel heard, comfortable, and supported. Learn more about our carers' Services or Contact us to discuss your needs.

Home care offers numerous benefits, including: Comfort and Familiarity: Staying at home allows individuals to remain in a familiar environment, which can be comforting and reduce stress. Personalized Care: Home care services are tailored to the specific needs and preferences of the individual, ensuring personalized attention. Maintaining Independence: Home care enables individuals to retain a sense of independence and control over their daily routines and lifestyle. Family Involvement: Loved ones can be more involved in the care process, providing emotional support and ensuring that care aligns with the individual's preferences. Cost-Effective: Home care can be more affordable than residential care facilities, especially when only part-time assistance is needed.

While carers can assist with a wide range of tasks, there are certain responsibilities they typically cannot perform, including: Medical Procedures: Carers are not usually qualified to perform complex medical procedures or administer medications that require specialized training. Heavy Lifting: Tasks involving heavy lifting or moving heavy objects may be outside a carer's scope to prevent injury. Financial Management: Handling the client’s finances, making financial decisions, or managing legal affairs are generally not part of a carer’s duties. Personal Errands: Running personal errands that do not directly relate to the client's care needs may not be included. Extensive House Maintenance: Major home repairs or extensive house maintenance tasks are not typically within a carer’s role. It’s important to clarify the specific duties and limitations with your care provider to ensure a clear understanding of what can and cannot be done.

At Mascon Care, we ensure that all our carers and support workers undergo thorough checks and screening before joining our team. We conduct comprehensive background checks, including verification of training, certificates, references, and relevant criminal records, to confirm their professionalism. Every caregiver participates in induction training upon starting and shadows a supervisor or manager before independently attending to clients. We also value client feedback on these shadowing shifts, as your opinion is crucial to us. If you have any concerns or feedback about your carer, please reach out to us, and we will be happy to discuss them with you.

Pricing, Payment and Funding Options

At Mascon Care, we recognize the uniqueness of every patient, which is why we offer personalized pricing plans to match your specific needs. Care costs can vary significantly based on the required service level and individual preferences. We provide a wide range of services, from one-hour weekly visits to 24-hour live-in care. Factors such as overnight care, medical requirements, social support, and carer-assisted transportation can also influence the cost. When creating your person-centred care plan, we consider the level of care, the duration and timing of services, and any additional needs or accommodations. Our goal is to keep our costs affordable to alleviate concerns for our clients and their families. For more detailed discussions, please contact us.

If you do not qualify for financial assistance from your local authority, you can still benefit from our services through private payment. We offer transparent pricing with a clear payment schedule and no hidden fees. Bills are issued at agreed intervals, such as monthly, and payments can be made via electronic transfer (BACS), cheque, or standing order. If your care requirements evolve, we are flexible to review your personalized care plan and provide updated pricing accordingly, at any stage. For any further inquiries regarding funding or payment choices, please get in touch with us directly.
